Productivity per translator

This table shows the translation speed for each individual test participant, in "extrapolated" words per hour. These numbers do not mean that participants actually translated that many words per hour but reflect the average per-segment speed. So they do not take into account breaks, idle time between segments nor any time translators may have spent re-reading their translations in context (whereas revisiting segments to make corrections is taken into account).
