Raw MT samples from the Autodesk productivity test

These raw MT samples are made available in response to requests from test participants because for some languages, our test used MT output produced with different systems. The samples provided here were only produced with the Moses engines Autodesk uses in production.

Note that specific issues which were left unaddressed in the productivity test are or will soon be addressed in our production workflow (e.g. placeholder positioning and the automatic generation of hotkeys for Asian languages).

  • Japanese samples
  • Korean samples

The samples are provided as tab-separated UTF-8 text files. If you open the files directly from within your browser you may have to choose the correct encoding.
